    21тЖТ    21тЖТ### What is this platform?
    22тЖТ    22тЖТ
    23тЖТ    23тЖТ**Infeworks** (domain: `infeworks.com`) is the digital presence of **International for Engineering Works** тАФ an Egyptian EPC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction) contractor headquartered in Naser City, Cairo.     24тЖТ    24тЖТ

    26тЖТ    26тЖТ

    47тЖТ    47тЖТ### Core Value Proposition
    48тЖТ    48тЖТ
    49тЖТ    49тЖТ**Infeworks bridges the gap between complex engineering execution and modern project management.** The company delivers turnkey solutions across three interconnected pillars:
    50тЖТ    50тЖТ
    51тЖТ    51тЖТ1. **Water & Wastewater Treatment** тАФ Design, supply, installation, and commissioning of water treatment plants, sewage treatment systems, industrial wastewater processing, UV filtration, and desalination support. This is the company's primary differentiator and revenue driver.
    52тЖТ    52тЖТ
    53тЖТ    53тЖТ2. **Electromechanical Systems** тАФ HVAC (heating, ventilation, air conditioning), fire fighting systems (wet/dry risers, sprinkler systems, FM200), steam and ventilation systems, and complete electrical panel engineering.
    54тЖТ    54тЖТ
    55тЖТ    55тЖТ3. **Civil & Infrastructure Construction** тАФ Excavation, earthworks, concrete works, road construction, and building structural works. This is the supporting capability that enables the company to deliver full EPC packages (not just equipment supply).
    56тЖТ    56тЖТ
    57тЖТ    57тЖТ**Why clients choose Infeworks over competitors:**
    58тЖТ    58тЖТ- **Single-source responsibility**: One contract covers design + procurement + construction + commissioning (EPC model), eliminating multi-vendor coordination headaches.
    59тЖТ    59тЖТ- **Cross-disciplinary capability**: The combination of water treatment expertise + electromechanical + civil works under one roof is rare in the Egyptian market. Most competitors specialize in only one domain.
    60тЖТ    60тЖТ- **15+ years of project history**: The website testimonials reference 5+ year relationships, indicating deep industry roots and repeat business.
    61тЖТ    61тЖТ- **MENA market coverage**: While based in Egypt, the company's capabilities (modular treatment units, containerized systems) are scalable to Gulf markets where water infrastructure demand is exploding ($2.04B тЖТ $4.72B by 2033).

   404тЖТ   404тЖТ### Brand Voice & Personality
   405тЖТ   405тЖТ
   406тЖТ   406тЖТ**Voice Attributes:**
   407тЖТ   407тЖТ| Attribute | Description | Do | Don't |
   408тЖТ   408тЖТ|---|---|---|---|
   409тЖТ   409тЖТ| **Confident** | Speaks with authority rooted in engineering expertise | Use active voice, definitive statements, quantified claims | Use tentative language ("we believe", "we think", "maybe") |
   410тЖТ   410тЖТ| **Precise** | Every word carries specific meaning тАФ no fluff | Use technical terms correctly, cite specific metrics | Use vague marketing jargon ("world-class", "best-in-class", "cutting-edge") |
   411тЖТ   411тЖТ| **Outcome-Oriented** | Leads with results and impact, not features | "We delivered a 500 m┬│/day treatment plant in 6 months" | "We provide high-quality water solutions" |
   412тЖТ   412тЖТ| **Modern** | Sounds like a Y-Combinator startup that moves concrete | Short sentences, clear hierarchy, modern formatting | Wall-of-text paragraphs, passive voice, bureaucratic tone |
   413тЖТ   413тЖТ| **Bilingual-Ready** | All content must work in both Arabic and English | Use simple sentence structures, avoid cultural idioms | Use English-specific wordplay or culturally-specific references |
   414тЖТ   414тЖТ
   415тЖТ   415тЖТ**Copywriting Guidelines:**
   416тЖТ   416тЖТ- Headlines: 6тАУ10 words maximum. Must communicate the outcome or value, not just the category. Example: "We Engineer Water Intelligence" not "Water Treatment Services"
   417тЖТ   417тЖТ- Body text: 3тАУ5 sentences per paragraph. Lead with the problem, then the solution, then the outcome. Every paragraph must pass the "So what?" test.
   418тЖТ   418тЖТ- CTAs: Action-oriented, specific. "Request a Project Consultation" not "Contact Us". "View Our Portfolio" not "Our Work".
   419тЖТ   419тЖТ- Arabic content: Use formal but accessible Arabic ( Fus'ha тАФ not overly classical, not colloquial). Equivalent technical terms must be accurate (e.g., ┘Е╪н╪╖╪з╪к ┘Е╪╣╪з┘Д╪м╪й ╪з┘Д┘Е┘К╪з┘З for "water treatment plants").
   420тЖТ   420тЖТ- Numbers: Always use numerals (15, not "fifteen") for statistics, capacities, and measurements. Include units (m┬│/day, kW, months, EGP/USD).
